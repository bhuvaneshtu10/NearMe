Tiruvannamalai Map

Tiruvannamalai is a historic temple town in the state of Tamil Nadu, India. It is world-renowned as a major spiritual and cultural center, attracting millions of pilgrims and tourists every year. The town lies at the foothills of the sacred Annamalaiyar Hill and is especially famous for the grand Arunachaleswarar Temple, one of the largest temples in India dedicated to Lord Shiva.
